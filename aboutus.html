ABOUT US

Our journey began in 1994 when a young boy's determination to support his family sparked an idea of creating wealth through property ownership. Recognizing the profound impact of not having a home, we set out to bridge this gap and empower individuals and families across the country. Today, with a wealth of industry experience and deep-rooted knowledge, we have become the first successful Mortgage Broker in Papua New Guinea.

At Asigau Mortgage Brokers, our primary goal is to close the gap and make property ownership accessible to all. We believe that having a home goes beyond financial benefits—it improves productivity, fosters well-being, and enhances overall quality of life. With a strong commitment to the PNG government's 2050 Vision of creating a wealthier, healthier, and smarter society, we are dedicated to collaborating with our clients and stakeholders to achieve our shared objectives.

Our approach is built on a foundation of shared information and resource allocation. We craft tailored solutions that resonate with you, taking into account your unique goals and budget. Our result-oriented framework ensures that we efficiently satisfy your housing needs, providing you and your family with rewarding and sustainable solutions.

As a reputable organization, we are proud to have positively impacted numerous individuals and organizations throughout our journey. With steady growth and stability, we are well-equipped to continue making a difference for years to come.

At Asigau Mortgage Brokers, we firmly believe that everyone deserves a home. We are committed to creating a comprehensive framework that culminates in a highly productive outcome, influencing positive growth in our society. Your housing aspirations are at the heart of what we do, and we are here to support you every step of the way.

Please do not hesitate to reach out to us if you have any questions or require further information. We are excited to be a part of your housing journey and look forward to helping you achieve your dreams.

Thank you for choosing Asigau Mortgage Brokers..
