The Board of Directors

Nou William Asigau

CEO & Chairman

Nou is the founding Director and the current Chief Executive Officer for Asigau Mortgage Brokers. He has a Bachelor with a Double Major in Entrepreneurship and International Management with over 15 years of Banking and more than 25 years in the Property Consulting Industry.

Having led, managed and worked in a variety of managing roles with challenging, complex and multi-disciplined organizations, his approach to business emphasises the need to empower our people with information to make smarter decisions to create wealth for a better alternative in life.

Willie Asigau

Non-Executive Diector

Willie is the Owner and Managing Director of Noho Namo Properties Limited. Having 35 years of experience and insight into the Construction and Real Estate Industry with a comfortable property portfolio.

A professional Environmentalist with a Masters in Marine Biology from the James Cook University of North Queensland, Australia, Willie has contributed to many development policies for Papua New Guinea such as the PNG 2050 Vision established in 2009. His insight both on development and the property industry brings a complementary dynamic into the leadership, strategic planning and operating of Asigau Mortgage Brokers.

Nanadai Asigau

Non-Executive Director

Nanadai is an established Banker with more than 50 years in Executive Management roles within the Banking and Finance Industry. His wealth of experience spans back to 1974 at the introduction of The National Australian Bank in Papua New Guinea.

An established profession, he has served on several Boards as Director for Commercial and Non-for-Profit Organizations. His industry knowledge in Banking and finance, complimented with his experience serving on multiple Boards in difference disciplines, brings strength and foresight to the organizations planning to better serve its customers into the future.
