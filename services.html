Initial Consultations

Our initial consultation is a high-profile discussion to establish your goals and the resources available to support it. It is also a place to establish what status you are in the market (First Home Buyer or Property Investor). This service offering is to understand your current position to ensure appropriate steps are taken to complement the endorsement of a home loan application that is compatible with your budget and your future plans.

Documentation

Documentation is a checklist of documents compiled and required to complement our presentation of a home/property loan proposal for funding and also ensure necessary due diligence is executed to mitigate any and all risks related to obtaining a home or property investment loan. Documentation service is triggered to satisfy the legal obligations of formal compliance to a binding agreement that also entails the declaration of terms and conditions that come with it.

Credit Assessments

Credit Assessment is a service we offer to customers who want to know their actual financial position, along with the loan facility and the lender they're compatible with. This service empowers customers in planning and decision making for a first home, growing their investment portfolio, refinancing, or restructuring their existing debt. Pre-Assessment is a financial analysis that verifies your current status and empowers you to decide on a future status you desire.

Client Establishments

Our service delivery is a systematic method in which to ensure we have checks and balances all throughout this process to increase the probability of a loan approval and owning of the desired property. The establishment phase of our service is the final stage of this methodical process. We introduce and establish our customers with the financial institution by migrating the customer's financial capabilities to ensure a harmonious relationship between the bank (lender) and customer from the introduction to acceptances as a customer of this commercial institution.
